Torna Fort, also known as Prachanda gad, is a large fort located in Pune district, in the Indian state of Maharashtra. 
It is historically significant because it was the first fort captured by Shivaji I in 1646, at the age of 16.


Malhargad is a hill fort in western India near Saswad, 30 kilometres from Pune. It is also known as Sonori Fort due 
to the village of Sonori being situated at its base. The fort was named for the god Malhari and was the last fort built by the Marathas, in 1775.



Vairatgad fort is a fort situated near to the town of Wai vyajwadi in Maharashtra, India. The top of the fort is very small. 
There are remnants of old buildings. The bastion at the entrance is dilapidated. 5-6 cisterns can be seen to the left of the main gate, on the lower side of the cliff.
